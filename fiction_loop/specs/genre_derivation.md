# GENRE DERIVATION — reference method (Stage 3 of intake_factory.spec.md)

How to derive a fiction genre from a knowledge book. Genre is DERIVED, not chosen:
the requirements pin the structure; the remaining freedom is the skin. Recorded from
the 2026-07-03 design conversation (owner + Claude), where this method reproduced the
owner's gate-fiction choice from first principles and rejected the alternatives he had
tried (detective) or floated (absurdist school slice-of-life with resets).

## Step 1 — Classify the knowledge by what MASTERY looks like

| Type | Mastery looks like | Fiction strategy |
|---|---|---|
| Thing (concepts, systems) | can't stop seeing the concept everywhere | concept becomes the WORLD'S PHYSICS (1.docx: dantian = single point of failure) |
| Process (methods) | executes moves under pressure, unnamed | experience-through-failure; bounded fair problem instances |
| Perception (taste, judgment) | discriminates what others can't see | contrast-pair worlds where seeing differently is power |
| Relational (negotiation etc.) | process runs on other minds | repeated varied encounters with beings whose responses are legible |
| Discipline (habits, training) | compounding over time | time-lapse structure (cultivation fits almost perfectly) |

## Step 2 — Extract the book's own failure catalog
Every good knowledge book documents how people get it wrong (Pólya's bad solvers →
the 14 wrong-approach types). The genre must make THOSE failures dramatizable with
physical consequences. Strictest filter — most candidates die here.

## Step 3 — Requirements (near-invariant; weight by type)
1. Problems playable WITHOUT domain expertise (reader must fail alongside the solver).
2. Guaranteed solvability — the reader's contract to keep trying (world_rules Rule 2).
3. Externalized mental state (thinking is invisible; something must show it).
4. Episodic variety + ensemble (curriculum × touches needs many problems, many solvers).
5. Adjacency to ordinary life (transfer proof — echoes need a real world next door).
6. Real, fair stakes (wrong approaches must cost).
7. Serial macro-mystery scaffold.

## Step 4 — Stress-test candidates; expect convergence
Reference kill-table (process knowledge, from the Pólya derivation):

| Genre | Dies on |
|---|---|
| Detective | #1 & #3: information asymmetry manufactures the reader's errors; genius protagonist reads as talent; the climax is literally an explanation — the anti-pattern |
| Medical / legal / heist / survival-engineering | #1: domain expertise substitutes for process |
| Death game / escape rooms | #5: sealed cast, no ordinary life to transfer into; gamemaster pulls toward outwitting a person |
| LitRPG / stat towers | spirit of all: numbers let readers optimize instead of think |
| Magic school | the deepest principle: school puts the name BEFORE the experience |
| Time loop | #4: one solver, one problem iterated (steal as a single-gate episode, not a genre) |
| Anomalous zones (Roadside Picnic) | #2: Zones are unfair by design |
| Spirit/folklore petition | pulls to parable — readers expect a stated moral (= explanation) |
| Absurdist school slice-of-life w/ resets | resets negate compounding — anti-Sankofa; comedy defuses the wrong approach into a punchline |

Convergence result for process books: any world with (a) bounded self-contained
problem spaces, (b) fair + guaranteed-solvable, (c) appearing inside ordinary life,
(d) lethal stakes, (e) a reflective surface for the solver's mind — IS gate fiction,
whatever the costume. Genres are skins over the requirement-skeleton.

## Step 5 — Invent the externalization mechanism (the custom step)
No genre catalog supplies it. For Sankofa it is the mirror/register mechanic (mental
frame → physical room arrangement) — the project's actual original invention, and the
engine the genre merely houses. This step is taste-bearing: draft candidates, render
taste flights, owner corrects (intake_factory Stage 6).

## Step 6 — Pick the skin with the most free infrastructure
Gate fiction brought guilds, grades, and response infrastructure for free — three
things the curriculum needed anyway. Then run the correspondence audit: IS not LIKE;
every element traces to a book mechanic with the same failure mode.

## Worked outputs (same method, different books — proves it derives, not backfits)

| Book | Type | Derived genre | Invented mechanism |
|---|---|---|---|
| How to Solve It | process | gates in ordinary life | the mirror (frame → room) |
| Atomic Habits | discipline | cultivation (compounding IS the qi loop; identity = realm) | visible accumulation physics w/ decay |
| Never Split the Difference | relational | creature-bonding (tactical empathy IS taming) | creature posture mirrors the last move |
| Thinking, Fast and Slow | perception/process | districts that each induce one cognitive distortion | environmental tells reader learns first |
| Design of Everyday Things | perception | appraiser/inspector fiction (misdesign is lethal) | the object's "invitation" made visible |
